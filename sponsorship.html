Why partner with us?

Dublin Sensual Festival provides a unique, emotionally engaging setting for brand visibility. Dancers attend for an entire weekend, staying present and connected a rare opportunity for meaningful engagement.

  • Reach 300+ attendees across 3 days
  • Audience: Students and professionals between the age of 18-65 with lifestyle and travel focus
  • Promote products through sampling, content, or branding
  • Link your brand with wellness, art, and positive community experiences

Extended visibility

Beyond the festival, our partner association hosts weekly non-alcoholic socials at quedadassalseras.com. These gatherings attract hundreds of recurring dancers ideal for continuous brand presence throughout the year.
