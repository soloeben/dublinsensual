How can I buy tickets? Tickets & Discounts

＋

All tickets are available through Eventbrite — click the Get Ticket button on our site and follow the steps.

Do you offer any discounts? Tickets & Discounts

＋

€20 off for International Guests: Travelling from outside Ireland? Book your travel, email a copy of your ticket or booking confirmation to quedadassalseras@gmail.com, and we’ll send you a discount link before you purchase.

Group discounts: Attending with friends? Contact us directly — we may have special group rates or perks.

Do you help with accommodation? Accommodation

＋

Yes. While we can’t guarantee placements, we try to connect guest dancers with local hosts from the Dublin community. To request assistance, email quedadassalseras@gmail.com or post in the event WhatsApp group.

Which dance genres are featured? Dance Styles & Program

＋

The festival includes workshops, socials and performances in Bachata and Salsa, led by international artists and DJs.

What types of passes are available? Dance Styles & Program

＋

Full Pass: access to all workshops & parties. Style-specific passes cover only their named genre plus some socials.

When and where does the event take place? Dates, Venue & Location

＋

The 2025 Festival runs from October 2–5 at St Vincent’s GAA, Malahide Rd, Marino, Dublin 3 (D03 P6C3). Note: Thursday’s opener is at Madigan’s, O’Connell Street, Dublin 1.

Where can I find directions or transport info? Dates, Venue & Location

＋

See the Venue page for live map links and transport notes from the airport and city centre. The printed guide will also include step-by-step directions.

What’s included with my ticket? Ticketing Terms

＋

Full Pass holders get access to all workshops, socials, performances, and live DJs. Style-specific passes are limited to their named genre plus selected socials.

Can I get a refund or transfer my ticket? Ticketing Terms

＋

All sales are final via Eventbrite. Resale or transfers are at the organizers’ discretion — email quedadassalseras@gmail.com with your ticket details and we’ll advise.

Is the event beginner-friendly? Any age restrictions? Ticketing Terms

＋

We welcome dancers of all levels, from absolute beginners to advanced. Please check workshop descriptions for levels. You must be 18+ to attend — please carry valid ID.

Do you offer sponsorship or brand collaboration options? Sponsorship & Collaboration

＋

Yes. Dublin Sensual Festival offers limited sponsorship and collaboration options for brands that want visibility in front of a fast-growing Latin dance community.

We are open to headline partners, category sponsors (fitness, wellness, fashion, travel, hospitality, etc.), and in-kind collaborations (products, services, prizes).

For a detailed sponsorship deck and current opportunities, please visit our Sponsorship page or email us at admin@dublinsensual.com.

What kind of brands are you looking to partner with? Sponsorship & Collaboration

＋

We prioritise brands that align with our values: community, health, and meaningful, alcohol-lite / alcohol-free social spaces.

Ideal partners include:

  • Gyms, sportswear and dancewear brands
  • Non-alcoholic drinks, healthy snacks and lifestyle brands
  • Travel, accommodation and local hospitality partners
  • Media, content and photography/video teams

Our partner association also runs weekly socials with hundreds of dancers, promoting non-alcoholic gatherings and healthy nightlife. Sponsors can gain ongoing visibility there as well as at the main festival.

Can local dance schools or communities collaborate with you? Sponsorship & Collaboration

＋

Absolutely. We are happy to collaborate with local and international dance schools, communities and event organizers.

Depending on the fit, this can include:

  • Group passes or affiliate codes for your students
  • Cross-promotion of your weekly socials or classes
  • Shared content, reels and community highlights around the festival

To discuss a collaboration, email admin@dublinsensual.com with a short intro about your school/community and how you’d like to partner.

How can I get real-time updates or ask questions? Staying Connected

Join our official WhatsApp group for announcements, accommodation coordination and to connect with attendees and hosts.

How do I contact you directly? Staying Connected
