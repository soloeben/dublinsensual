Accommodation Assistance

We aim to support guest dancers by connecting them with local hosts from the Dublin community where possible. This is a voluntary matching effort—placements are not guaranteed and depend on host availability.

Need help? Email the organizing team at quedadassalseras@gmail.com with your travel dates, preferred room type, and any special requirements.

  • Priority may be given to artists, competitors, or overseas travelers.
  • We’ll share a WhatsApp group link to coordinate hosts and guests closer to the event.

Stay Safe & Comfortable

  • Confirm arrival times with hosts and share your flight number when relevant.
  • Respect quiet hours—many hosts live with families or flatmates.
  • If a match isn’t available, use the Nearby Amenities for hotel/hostel ideas.

How to get to the venue

Below are typical options. Always check TFI Journey Planner for live routes and times.

From

Dublin Airport (DUB)

Taxi / Ride-hail

Door-to-door in ~20–35 mins depending on traffic.

Express Coach

Airport coaches run to the city (e.g., Drumcondra / O’Connell St). From there, take a short bus/ride-hail to Marino.

Public Bus

Regular buses run from the airport towards the north city. Use the TFI planner to pick the fastest combo to Marino / Malahide Rd.

From

Dublin City Centre

Bus

Frequent services head towards Malahide Rd / Fairview / Marino. The stop nearest the venue is a short walk.

Train + Bus

Commuter rail to Clontarf Road or Drumcondra, then hop on a local bus or walk/ride-hail.

Cycle / Walk

From the north inner city, it’s cycle-friendly via Fairview Park and Marino. Walking from Fairview is ~15–20 mins.

Parking & Access

  • Limited parking may be available at/near the venue. Street parking rules apply—please obey local signage.
  • Arrive early for evening socials as nearby streets can get busy.
  • The main halls are at ground level. If you have accessibility needs, email us in advance and we’ll assist.
