Nearby Amenities

Cafes & Restaurants

There are several cafes and restaurants within a short walking distance of the venue, offering a variety of options for meals and snacks.

Hotels & Accommodation

A range of hotels and B&Bs are available in the surrounding area to suit different budgets. Early booking is recommended.

Parking & Access

The venue has a dedicated car park, but space is limited. Street parking is available in the vicinity, but please check local restrictions. The area is well-served by public transport, which is the recommended mode of travel.

Venue Gallery

Accommodation Assistance

We aim to support guest dancers by connecting them with local hosts. This is a voluntary effort and placements are not guaranteed.

Need help? Email admin@dublinsensual.com with your travel dates and requirements.

Nearby Hotels & Hostels

For convenience, look for stays in the Marino, Fairview, or Drumcondra areas. These offer a mix of hotels, B&Bs, and are a short bus ride or walk from the venue.

Getting Here from Dublin Airport (DUB)

A taxi or ride-hail will be the quickest (~20-35 mins). Alternatively, airport coaches run to the city (Drumcondra / O’Connell St), from where you can take a short bus or taxi to Marino.

Getting Here from City Centre

Frequent buses head towards Malahide Rd / Fairview / Marino. Use the TFI Journey Planner for live routes. From the north inner city, it’s also cycle-friendly.
